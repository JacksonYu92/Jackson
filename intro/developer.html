How I start

In early 2020, the pandemic lockdown has created free time for me to get into web development. ZERO experience in programming prior. I continued coding little by little, getting stuck a lot and even thinking that maybe coding wasn't for someone like me. I made my first ever HTML page in April. Then, I learned about CSS and JavaScript from online courses on Udemy.

Challenge

Being consistent with study is tough when life back to normal, especially when I worked for two jobs at the same time. I've pushed hard for myself to make a new habit of waking up before 5 o'clock in the morning. Do 2 ~ 3 hours of study before work and 1 ~ 2 hours of study at night for every Monday to Friday. I tried to learn as much as I could over the weekend. I am constantly pushing myself out of my comfort zone to learn more and hopefully make my dream come true one day in the future. As a full-stack developer, I enjoy learning new stuff daily and building amazing things.
