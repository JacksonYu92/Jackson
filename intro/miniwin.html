Miniwin Electronic Commerce

Miniwin Electronic Commerce was founded and launched their first project windiscount.ca(a local discount information website) on Oct, 2013.

At the beginning, company focused on providing local discount information to Chinese community in Winnipeg, Canada. We've tried collecting discount information daily from each local stores(included Superstore, Costco, Bestbuy, &etc), then share and post daily on Weibo (a Chinese microblogs, similar to Twitter). We also collect & share local events, fun places for hangout, restaurants that must try, and any other such information to local Chinese community.
