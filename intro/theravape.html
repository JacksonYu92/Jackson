I've spent most my life at TheraVape Inc.

TheraVape Inc. is one of Canada's premier E-liquid manufacturers, founded in 2013. They also have two retail stores and e-commerce.

Company's mission is to help professionalize the vape industry with our core values in order to keep smokers switching to a less harmful alternative, while helping combat and prevent youth uptake.

They're also one of the only ISO9001:2015 accredited eLiquid manufacturers in Canada.
